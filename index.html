What is Coronavirus?

Coronaviruses is a group of viruses that recognized by an informal group of virologists with the appearance, recalling the solar corona under the electron microscope. In humans they're typically spread via airborne droplets of fluid produced by infected individuals. Today, the coronavirus disease is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.

  • Official name is Covid-19 (previously known as "2019 novel coronavirus", where "novel" means new) given by World Health Organization (WHO).
  • The novel human coronavirus disease COVID-19 has become the fifth documented pandemic since the 1918 flu pandemic.
  • The virus called severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) because it is genetically related to the coronavirus responsible for the SARS outbreak of 2003.
  • Identified in Year 2019, following a report of a cluster of cases of ‘viral pneumonia’ in Wuhan, People’s Republic of China.

How To Protect Yourself

You should do

  • Stay at home
  • Wear mask
  • Use Sanitizer
  • Disinfect your home
  • Wash your hands
  • Frequent self-isolation

You should avoid

  • Avoid infected people
  • Avoid animals
  • Avoid handshaking
  • Aviod infected surfaces
  • Don't touch your face
  • Avoid droplets

Symptoms of Coronavirus

Be alert with these symptoms that anyone have possible to be infected...

High Fever

Exceed 37.8 degree celcius and above, commonly occur between 2 days to 14 days after you have been exposed to the virus

Cough

Continuous cough, loss of/change in, sense of smell or taste (anosmia), difficultly breathing

Sore Troath

Feel painful, dry or scratchy in the throat, occurs sometimes

Headache

Be moderately to severely painful and resistant to regular painkillers, last for more than three days
